The reason why you should use Dart with Flutter, instead of the followings in our your development

The bad of others:

  • Java: Popular, but a bit old, confusing and cumbersome.
  • Kotlin: Good in general, but requires knowledge from Java
  • Swift: Single platform. No good if you want a cross-platform app
  • Android: Single platform.
  • Javascript: Confusing variable scope. No good for an app design
  • Ruby: Lack of libs and components.
  • PHP: Good to learn. But it is more about network programming
  • Go: Good in general. Advancing quickly. Requires some time to learn.
  • Python: Low performance. Easy to learn but not good for app development.
  • C++: Good to learn. But it does not have good supports for cross-platform.
  • C#: Same as above.
  • Lua: Bad ecosystem. Same as python.

The good of Dart

  • Has a very powerful framework - Flutter for cross-platform development.
  • It's a new-gen language - simple syntax; easy to learn.
  • Single-threaded. Meaning there are no locks, and no usual lags from JVM (especially Android).
  • It supports both JIT and AOT..
  • Cross-platform. Imagine using one language for both Android and IOS!
  • No more Java-styled stinky, inflexible, long, annoying code.
  • Good support from both community and Google.
